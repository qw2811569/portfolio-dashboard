Paydex
Passport to borderless money management and international finance
✺ Overview
Mobile App that empowers sending money to loved ones, paying international bills, or managing business finances
With a user base exceeding 200,000 and transaction volumes surpassing $450 million within the first 12 months, Paydex revolutionized the way individuals and businesses manage their finances across borders
Client
NDA Fintech Co.
London, The UK
Services
UX Design, UI Design
✺ Challenge
The primary challenge was to integrate a wide array of financial services, from currency exchange to international transfers, demanding a balance between clarity and depth of information
Overcoming these challenges required close collaboration with stakeholders, extensive research, and a deep understanding of the nuances of international finance, ultimately resulting in the creation of a robust and user-centric international banking mobile app
✺ Features
Paydex is more than just a banking app—it's a gateway to a world of financial freedom and opportunity
Borderless Account Payees allows users to hold and manage money in multiple currencies, making international transactions seamless
Currency Exchange We designed the currency conversion process to be straightforward and quick. Users can select the currencies to exchange and instantly see the converted amount at the current rate
Money Transfers Feature is designed with simplicity in mind, enabling users to initiate a transfer in just a few taps
Transaction Insights We integrated several data visualization tools to enable users to quickly understand their financial information
✺ Solution
Successfully created an international mobile banking app that meets the diverse needs of a global user base while ensuring security, transparency, and ease of use
50+ currencies supported
50,000+ borderless accounts created
21% monthly growth rate in user sign-ups
59% user retention rate after six months
