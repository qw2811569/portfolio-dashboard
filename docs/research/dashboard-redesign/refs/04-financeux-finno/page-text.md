Finno
Gateway to secure, simple, and fast transactions
✺ Overview
Finno is a next-generation digital payment platform, designed to meet the evolving needs of today’s users
Finno, inspired by leading fintech platforms, was developed to bridge the gap between convenience and trust in digital payments. Since its launch, our app has received positive feedback for its simplicity, security, and unique cashback rewards system. It has attracted a growing user base, including individuals, freelancers, and small businesses
Client
NDA Fintech Co.
Atlanta, Georgia
Services
UX Design, UI Design
✺ Challenge
The main challenge is to build a platform that stands out in the competitive financial technology landscape
With the increasing reliance on online shopping, freelance work, and global business interactions, users need a trustworthy platform that handles transactions securely while offering additional value. Existing solutions, while effective, often leave room for improvement in user experience, international payments, and customized services. Our team focused on creating an intuitive user interface that simplifies complex financial transactions
✺ Features
Finno acts as a digital wallet, providing customers with a convenient and secure way to handle their finances across various currencies and regions
Instant Money Transfers Users can send money to friends and family quickly and easily using their Finno balance, bank accounts, or linked cards
One-Touch Payments Finno allows users to make purchases online with ease, supporting a wide range of merchants across the globe
Finno Cashback Users can redeem their cashback directly into their Finno account, where it can be used for online purchases, money transfers, or transferred to a linked bank account
Cryptocurrency Support Finno allows users to buy, sell, and hold cryptocurrencies within the Finno app. This feature offers a secure and simple way for users to engage with cryptocurrencies
‍
✺ Solution
By focusing on user-centric design, we have successfully created a digital payment platform that meets the needs of modern users
Finno stands out not only for its functionality but also for the thoughtfulness of its design, which makes complex financial transactions simple and secure. As we continue to evolve the platform, we remain committed to enhancing user experience and expanding our feature set to keep pace with the ever-changing digital landscape
