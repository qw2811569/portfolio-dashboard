Quicked 7
Business accounting platform
✺ Overview
Quicked 7 enables users to create and customize professional invoices, track expenses automatically through bank feeds, and process payroll with automated tax calculations
Launched in January 2024, Quicked 7 has swiftly established itself as the essential platform for businesses aiming to streamline their financial management and drive significant growth
‍
Client
Stealth Fintech Startup
Los Angeles, California
Services
UX Design, UI Design
✺ Challenge
Creating an app that meets the diverse needs of businesses while providing a seamless and efficient user experience
Orion Corporation, a business services provider, faced challenges in managing their invoicing processes due to outdated systems and manual workflows. To streamline their operations and enhance accuracy, they decided to develop a new digital platform specifically for managing invoices. Quicked 7 should be more than just software—it's a pivotal resource for modern businesses committed to financial excellence and operational success
‍
✺ Features
With a focus on usability, automation, and secure payment processing, Quicked 7 enhances efficiency, accuracy, and transparency
User-Friendly Dashboard Platform provides real-time updates on financial data, ensuring users have the latest information at their fingertips
Invoice Creation Allows users to set up recurring invoices for regular customers, automating the billing process. It also offers a variety of professional invoice templates that can be customized with branding elements
Automated Workflows Users can automate repetitive tasks such as invoice approvals, payment reminders, and expense categorization as well as create custom workflow rules to match their specific business processes
Payment Processing Allows users to track payment status in real-time, offering visibility into which invoices have been paid and which are outstanding. Quicked 7 sends automated reminders to clients for upcoming or overdue payments, improving cash flow
‍
✺ Solution
Quicked 7 offers a range of tools and features to simplify invoicing, payroll, and financial reporting
With its comprehensive features and user-friendly interface, Quicked 7 helps businesses save time, reduce errors, and gain valuable insights into their financial health, enabling them to focus on growth and success
