## **Client Pain**

The client’s legacy monitoring system relied on fragmented dashboards and manual checks, slowing response to pump failures. Data lacked real-time visibility, causing downtime, revenue loss, and poor control. The platform couldn’t scale with growing operations.

> 🖼 [image position=2] 3200×2400 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/47225452/file/74bd5b31a24918e2d5933dfc29aab39e.png`

## **About Project**

StreamLogic is our industrial SaaS platform that helps us monitor and manage pump systems in real time. The dashboard gives clear analytics, alerts, and AI-driven insights, allowing us to act faster, reduce downtime, and keep operations under full control.

> 🖼 [image position=4] 3200×2400 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/47225450/file/d399d807f5d251c18d45b553615d3bc9.png`

## **Business Task to Solve**

The client needed to replace a fragmented monitoring system with a centralized dashboard platform to gain real-time control over pump operations. Manual checks, delayed alerts, and poor data structure slowed decisions and limited scalability.

> 🖼 [image position=6] 3200×2400 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/47225451/file/c5aabb7df1562024d6d741f7469ed4ae.png`

## **Project Timeline & Process**

The project moved from audit and data mapping to system architecture and dashboard design. The deepest work was rebuilding logic and flows to unify fragmented data. Time was spent on analytics and structure to ensure scalable, real-time operations.

> 🖼 [image position=8] 3200×2400 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/47225453/file/eb15e5a4b9e189f96b241267001aacea.png`

## **Key Decisions**

We centralized all pump data into a single dashboard and introduced real-time alerts with priority logic. Based on usage analysis, we simplified flows and reduced noise, enabling faster decisions, better control, and a system that scales with growing operations.

> 🖼 [image position=10] 3200×2400 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/47225456/file/905fec9b7d269e3b30d1902f9b0b30ac.png`

## **Business Growth Effect**

Operational efficiency improved by 32%, and response time to failures became 2x faster. The system simplified monitoring and scaled across new sites, giving full transparency and control. “Now we see everything instantly and act without delays.”
