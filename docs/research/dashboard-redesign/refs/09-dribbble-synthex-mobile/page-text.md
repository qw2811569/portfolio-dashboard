## **Client Pain**

Synthex came with a bottleneck: data was scattered across tools, slowing decisions and hiding where revenue leaked. Their old dashboard couldn’t scale—operators lost time, teams lost control, and mobile workflows stalled.

> 🖼 [image position=2] 4800×3600 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/46735495/file/43169126fdffc7d22f14b97c2db951fb.png`

## **About Project**

"Our product is an AI-enhanced analytics app that centralizes metrics into one mobile dashboard. It gives us faster insights, cleaner data flows, and the operational control we need to grow without slowing down."

> 🖼 [image position=4] 4800×3600 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/46735496/file/5beab29c812bf8ac0486d31d84cc60f9.png`

## **Business Task to Solve**

The client needed to rebuild their analytics process: reduce manual reporting, speed up decisions, and centralize fragmented metrics. The task was to create a system and dashboard that restored control and supported scalable operations.

> 🖼 [image position=6] 4800×3600 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/46735497/file/055bea2f3ffbddf2ac7ea1770e79f75d.png`

## **Project Timeline & Process**

The project moved through research, data mapping, UX flows, and dashboard architecture. Most time went into reorganizing logic and analytics structures — this is where real value formed before moving to interface design and validation.

> 🖼 [image position=8] 4800×3600 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/46735499/file/98ee65fb033bd40126ece452fb804fd8.png`

## **Business Growth Effect**

Decision speed increased by 38%, reporting time dropped, and team efficiency improved across operations. The business gained more control and transparency in daily workflows.

Client: “Now decisions happen faster and with confidence.”
