## Designer's case-study text（依原 position 排序）

> 🖼 [image position=0] 3200×2400 → `https://cdn.dribbble.com/userupload/47524854/file/06c3941f1c33e7a9f77dc8f1e95c93ac.png`

This project is a design concept for a modern health tracking platform that helps users better understand and manage their daily well-being. The goal was to create a calm, intuitive, and insight-driven experience that makes personal health data feel accessible rather than overwhelming.

**We focused on:**

• Clear and structured health overview with key metrics at a glance

• Visual storytelling through charts, trends, and soft data visualization

• Minimal and calming interface to reduce cognitive load

• Consistent component system for scalability across dashboard and mobile

• Seamless transition between overview, detailed insights, and real-time tracking

The challenge was to balance data density with simplicity - presenting multiple health indicators like heart rate, sleep quality, and activity without overloading the user. The final design introduces a soft, nature-inspired visual language, smooth interactions, and a system that feels both personal and easy to navigate
